I build infrastructure, process, and pipelines that scale, for both people and tools.

I started in Quality Management Systems, where I learned how to turn messy process into something people could rely on. I carried that same instinct into documentation.

At one company, I became the solo technical writer within weeks. I owned the full CI/CD pipeline and made sure our API docs matched spec rules from development all the way through to production.

At the next company, I joined during a team restructuring. I ended up owning the platform end to end, improving how docs moved through the team, and training others on new ways of working.

Cross-functional collaboration is where I do my best work. I've onboarded engineering and writing teams onto new tools, partnered with product managers on content and release strategy, and mentored a junior technical writer to independently own our release cycle. Good infrastructure isn't just the pipeline itself. It's about giving every team around it a clear, easy way to work.

My work sits at the intersection of documentation infrastructure, docs ops, and AI.
