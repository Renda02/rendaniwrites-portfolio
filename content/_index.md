I build infrastructure, process, and pipelines that scale, for both people and tools.

I started in Quality Management Systems, where I learned how to turn unstructured process into something people could rely on. I carried that same instinct into documentation.

In my first role as a technical writer, I became solo within 3 weeks. I owned and maintained the documentation lifecycle within a continuous integration and deployment(CI/CD) pipeline in an agile environment.

In my current role, I joined during a team restructuring and ended up owning the documentation platform end to end, migrating us onto a new authoring tool and training both the team and PMs to work within it.

Good infrastructure is about giving every team using it a clear, easy way to work.

## What I do

- Own the documentation platform: architecture, tooling, infrastructure, and deployment workflows.
- Maintain integrations that keep docs in lockstep as features ship.
- Write and maintain API documentation, how-to guides, AI model cards, and integration documentation.
- Design localization pipelines that scale with product needs.
